A few words for our
Company
More

In our website you will find:

  • Anodes, they are the most important parts of the boat's cathodic protection. TECNOSEAL is one of the world’s leading suppliers of sacrificial anodes for cathodic protection, and incorporates the fundamental characteristics of know-how, top raw materials, combined with more than 30 years of experience, resulting in the widest range of anodes in the world. Our company, with a number of more than 500 codes in permanent and sufficient stock, guarantees that your needs will be met to the fullest.
  • Coatings, the right choice of paint whether it is for use under water (antifouling) or above water, is important. With us, all you need to do is choose the company that meets your needs, and we can cover you entirely.
  • Cleaning products, we can offer you a wide range of specially designed products for cleaning and protecting your boat. World-renowned brands such as Clinazur, Ma-Fra, 3M, Shurhold, Teak Wonder, T-Brite and many others guarantee to provide you with the optimal solution in any situation.
  • Fenders manufactured by CASTRO. A market leader with decades of experience,  make high quality products that will keep your boat safe. For fender covers, FENDRESS offers solutions for all types of fenders. The same company also produces the MEGA FENDER series. They are made of Hypalon fabric (same as that used in inflatable boats) with unique characteristics in terms of size-weight ratio, durability and minimal storage space.

 You will also find anchors, chains, windlasses, mooring and sailing ropes,  lifesaving equipment, maintenance materials, pumps and fittings, inflatable boats, towables  and SUPs, these are just a part of the large and ever-growing product range.
